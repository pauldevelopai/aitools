Why Grounded Exists

Organisations everywhere are at an inflection point. The challenge is no longer access to Artificial Intelligence, but the governance of it. Research shows that teams across newsrooms, NGOs, law firms and businesses are already using "Shadow AI" — unapproved tools adopted without oversight — to draft content, transcribe meetings, analyse data and generate code.

Your teams are likely already using AI tools. The danger is that they are using them blindly — feeding sensitive data into opaque models, risking confidentiality and relying on "black box" algorithms. Grounded is designed to move your organisation from ad-hoc AI usage to structured, ethical implementation. We don't merely list software. Our goal is to develop professionals who are not just users of technology, but auditors of it.

AI is already embedded in how organisations work, whether it is formally acknowledged or not. The question is therefore not whether AI should be adopted, but how it should be understood, governed and used responsibly — with the right ethics policies, legal frameworks and implementation strategies in place.
